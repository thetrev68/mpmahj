## STANDARD BASED ON EIGHT FLOWERS AND EIGHT JOKERS

Run-any consecutive numbers,Pair-2 tiles like ea other, Pung-3, Kong-4, Quint-5;Sextet-6.1 color-any 1 suit; 2 colors any 2 suits, 3colors any 3 suits,F--Flower,X-Exposed,C-Concealed,D-Dragon; Matching Dragons-Craks/Reds Bams/Greens Dots/Whites(Soaps)

All TILES ARE FACE DOWN, MIXED AND WALLSBUILT. EAST ROLLS DICE and total number thrown designates where East breaks wall then picks first tile and third top tiles and other players one tile each.

CHARLESTON-Any 3 tiles may be passed, with the exception of Jokers.
<div style="margin-left: 2em;">
First Charleston (compulsory)-three passes(right, across, left) Charleston may be stopped only after first left. Courtesy Pass still applies.<br>
Second Charleston(optional)-three passes (left,across, right)<br>
Blind pass of 1,2 or 3 tiles permitted on first left and/or last right.<br>
Courtesy Pass (optional) 0, 1, 2 or 3 tiles exchanged with opposite player.<br>
</div>
<br>
GAME BEGINS with East discarding 14th tile. Players to the right of East pick and discard in turn. Jokers may be discarded and named "Joker", "same", or name of previous discard. jokers may be used in place of any tile(s) in any Pung, Kong or Quint. Jokers may NEVER be used in place of a single tile or in any part of a pair. When a tile is in an exposure with like tile(s) whether picked from the wall or in player's hand, Joker(s) may be replaced in any exposure with like tile(s) whether picked from the wall or in player's hand. A player's turn begins when they either pick or claim a tile.

### CLAIMING A DISCARD

1. A tile may not be Exposure or Mah Jongg after player next in turn has picked and racked or discarded a tile.
2. A player must verbalize their call, alerting the other players that a claim is being made. The caller may say "call,""take," I want that," etc.
3. When two players want the same discard, one player for an Exposure and another for Mah Jongg, Mah Jongg declarer always has preference, even if callers tiles have been exposed.
4. If there is a simultaneous call for Exposure, the player next in turn gets preference.
   5.When two players want the same tile for Exposure, player next in turn to discarder has preference except when other caller has exposed tile(s).
5. When two players want the same tile for Mah Jongg, player next in turn to discarder has preference, except when other caller has exposed tile(s).

## PLAYERS SHOULD NOT THROW IN HANDS UNTIL MAH JONGG IS VERIFIED

MISNAMED TILE: A tile cannot be claimed untile correctly named. The correctly named tile may then be called for an Exposure. If Exposure is made with an incorrectly named tile, the hand is "dead."Misnamer is not penalized. However, if Mah Jongg is called with the incorrectly named tile, the game ceases and misnamer pays claimant 4times the value of the hand. Others do not pay.

A hand is considered to be "dead" when it has either too few or too many tiles or contains an incorrect exposure. The player whose hand has been declared "dead" must still continue to pick and discard, but cannot be winner the same as other players.

JOKERS may be exchanged from any and all exposures that were made before the hand was declared "dead."

Any tile, except a Joker, may be called for Mah Jongg, including Concealed and Singles and Pairs hands.

### MAH JONGG IN ERROR

1. If a player declares Mah Jongg in error and does not expose the hand and all other hands are intact, play continues without penalty. If all or part of the hand is exposed and hand is in error, declarer's hand is "dead."

2. If a player declares Mah Jongg in error and one other player exposes part or all of their hand, the game continues with the two remaining players whose hands are intact. If more than one player, other than the declarer, exposes part or all of their hand,the game cannot continue. Erring declarer pays double the value of the incorrect hand to the one player whose hand is intact.

3. If there are 3"dead" hands which did not result from Mah Jongg in error, then the surviving player throws in their hand and nobody gets paid.
